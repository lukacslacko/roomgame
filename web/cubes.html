What is this?

Server keeps a coarse 3D grid of cubes (default 50 cm). While recording, every frame's depth map updates per-cube counters. Cubes whose surface gets observed often enough are rendered as boxes overlaid on the camera. Use the mix slider to fade the cubes against the camera passthrough; switch to "occluded" mode to hide cubes that the live depth says are behind a real surface.
